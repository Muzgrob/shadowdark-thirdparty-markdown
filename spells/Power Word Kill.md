*Tier 5, wizard*

**Duration:** Instant
**Range:** Near

You utter the Word of Doom.

One creature you target of LV 9 or less dies if it hears you.

Treat a failed spellcasting check for this spell as a critical failure, and roll the mishap with disadvantage.
